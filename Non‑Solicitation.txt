Non‑Solicitation Clause
1. Purpose
This Non‑Solicitation Clause protects the business relationships, confidential information, and goodwill of Mountain Home Air Compressor (“Licensor”) without restricting lawful employment or competition.

2. Restricted Solicitation
During the term of this Agreement and for two (2) years following termination or expiration, Licensee shall not, directly or indirectly:

a. Customers & End Users
Solicit, divert, or attempt to solicit any customer, end user, or prospective customer of Licensor
Use information gained through the Software to target or service such customers with competing tools or services
b. Distributors & Partners
Solicit or induce any distributor, reseller, contractor, or business partner of Licensor to reduce or terminate their relationship with Licensor
c. Employees & Contractors
Solicit, recruit, or hire any employee, contractor, or service technician of Licensor
3. Use of Software Knowledge
Licensee may not use:

Diagnostic logic
Workflow structure
Troubleshooting methodology
Proprietary system design
obtained through the Software to solicit or service Licensor’s customers or partners outside the scope of this Agreement.

4. Permitted Activities
This clause does not prohibit Licensee from:

Working in the compressed air or industrial equipment industry
Providing mechanical or electrical service work
Developing unrelated software or tools
Competing generally, provided no solicitation occurs
5. Reasonableness & Severability
The parties agree that:

The scope and duration are reasonable
Any unenforceable portion shall be modified to the maximum extent permitted by law
The remainder shall remain fully enforceable
6. Remedies
Licensee acknowledges that a violation would cause irreparable harm.

Licensor is entitled to:

Injunctive relief
Recovery of reasonable attorney’s fees and costs
7. Survival
This Non‑Solicitation Clause survives termination of the Software License Agreement.
